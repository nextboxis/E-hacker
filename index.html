⚠️ Ethical Responsibility & Legal Disclaimer

Ethical hacking and security research must only be performed on networks and systems for which you have explicit, written authorization. Unauthorized access or scanning of computing resources is illegal and punishable under various cybersecurity laws worldwide (such as the Computer Fraud and Abuse Act in the US). Practice your skills inside guided sandbox laboratories like Hack The Box or TryHackMe, and use what you learn to protect, not exploit.
